01 — about

I'm an MIS Executive by profession at SeSTA (Seven Sisters Development Assistance), where I design and maintain programme-level MIS that field teams across Assam, Meghalaya, Arunachal Pradesh, and Tripura use to track activities, report on outcomes, and manage grant readiness. That spans programme data, MEL reporting, and the database structures that power them.

Before SeSTA, I worked as a System Administrator at Don Bosco College, which is where most of my Linux and infrastructure habits come from. I hold an MCA from Dibrugarh University. I'd rather ship a correct, direct solution than iterate endlessly — that applies to code and to the systems I design.
