01 — about

I'm an MIS Executive by profession at SeSTA (Seven Sisters Development Assistance), where I design and maintain the systems that NGOs and field teams across Northeast India use to track programmes, report on outcomes, and manage grant readiness. That spans everything from MIS data and reporting to the Laravel applications and raw SQL queries that power them.

Before SeSTA, I worked as a System Administrator at Don Bosco College, which is where most of my Linux and infrastructure habits come from. I hold an MCA from Dibrugarh University. I'd rather ship a correct, direct solution than iterate endlessly — that applies to code and to the systems I design.
